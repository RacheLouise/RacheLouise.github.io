About Us

Family Link deliver fun and educational activities for children and young people with complex disabilities, and we are looking for playworkers to join our team. We provide Saturday and school holiday activities which can be accessed by children with needs including, but not limited to, Autism, severe Learning Disabilities, Physical Disabilities and/or complex Health Needs. The children enjoy art, musical and creative activities, have the use of great facilities, including ball pools and a sensory room, and days trips to places of interest.
